NOUN Staff Rating Portal - Benin Study Centre

About the Portal

The NOUN Staff Rating Portal is an internal platform designed to gather constructive, anonymous, and authenticated feedback on teaching and professional staff. It aggregates ratings across configurable criteria, produces department and individual reports, and helps leadership identify strengths and areas for improvement.

Mission

To provide a transparent, fair, and actionable feedback system that supports professional development, teaching quality, and operational excellence at the National Open University of Nigeria, Benin Study Centre.

Vision

To embed continuous improvement into academic culture through timely insights and trusted reporting that promote growth and informed decision-making.

How it Works

Staff and administrators submit ratings based on defined criteria. Administrators configure the criteria and manage users. Reports summarize scores by department or individual for analysis.

Design Principles

  • Respectful feedback and evidence-based ratings
  • Privacy protection and consent-driven participation
  • Actionable reporting with clear insights
  • Accessibility and mobile responsiveness
