University Staff Rating Portal

About the Portal

The University Staff Rating Portal is an internal platform designed to gather constructive, anonymous and authenticated feedback on teaching and professional staff. The portal aggregates ratings across configurable criteria, produces department and individual reports, and helps leadership identify strengths and areas for improvement.

Mission

To provide a transparent, fair and actionable feedback system that supports professional development, teaching quality and operational excellence across the institution.

Vision

To embed continuous improvement into the academic culture by offering timely insights and trusted reporting that help people grow and decisions be data-informed.

How it works

Students and staff with permission submit ratings on defined criteria. Administrators configure criteria and manage users. Reports aggregate scores by individual, department and period and can be exported for further analysis.

Design principles

  • Respectful feedback: focus on constructive comments and evidence-based ratings
  • Privacy and consent: anonymize responses where required and protect personal data
  • Actionable reporting: concise summaries and drill-down details for decisions
  • Accessibility: keyboard-first controls and clear visual hierarchy
